The Tower of Four Arches (Hyderabad)

The Charminar, located in the heart of Hyderabad, is an iconic 16th-century monument known for its stunning Indo-Islamic architecture. Built by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah to commemorate the founding of the city, it features four grand arches and towering minarets. The Charminar is surrounded by bustling markets, offering a blend of history, culture, and vibrant street life. Its intricate design and historical significance make it one of the most recognized landmarks of Hyderabad.

The Path of Glory(Delhi)

Rajpath, located in the heart of New Delhi, is a ceremonial boulevard that holds immense historical and national significance. Stretching from the Rashtrapati Bhavan to India Gate, it is the site of grand events such as the Republic Day Parade, showcasing India's cultural diversity and military strength. Lined with beautiful gardens, fountains, and historic monuments, Rajpath offers a glimpse into India's colonial past and its journey to independence. It remains a symbol of national pride and unity.
